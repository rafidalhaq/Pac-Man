Title: Pac Man duplicate
Description: Similar to original Pac Man game, but different layout and different options. Collect a fruit for a bonus life, and complete a level for another life. The speeds are adjustable, and there is an option coded in to double Pac man's speed, or to double a random ghost's speed for a challenging game. Includes local High score tracking. Original artwork and coding.
